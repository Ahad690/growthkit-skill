[fiverr-gig-optimizer](https://github.com/Ahad690/fiverr-gig-optimizer) (the family's
lead star-bet) and [AppScope](https://github.com/Ahad690/open-app-intel). See
`../STAR_PLAYBOOK_from_career-ops.md` for the strategy.

## Positioning

The audience (SaaS founders) is narrower than fiverr's — so lean on the **cross-sell**:
"running AppScope too? its download estimate feeds GrowthKit's organic-attribution band
as an extra owned signal." The family-of-honest-tools story is stronger than any single
repo pitched alone.
