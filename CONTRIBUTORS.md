# Contributors

GrowthKit is open source (code: MIT; data/docs: CC-BY-4.0). Thanks to everyone
who contributes code, documentation, or anonymized public trend/benchmark data.

## Code & docs
- _Add yourself here when you open a PR._

## Federated dataset (`Ahad690/growthkit-trends`)
Contributors who share **public, anonymized** trend/benchmark rows via
`scripts/federation/contribute.py`. Only the shareable schema (see
[DATA_POLICY.md](DATA_POLICY.md)) ever leaves a contributor's machine — owned
analytics, handles, and any identifying field are blocked by
`assert_public_only`.

- _Add yourself here when your contribution PR is merged._
